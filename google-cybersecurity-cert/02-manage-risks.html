Course 2: Manage Security Risks

Course Overview

This course focused on understanding and managing cybersecurity risks in organizational settings. It emphasized the importance of risk management frameworks, business continuity, and security assessments in reducing vulnerabilities.

Module 1: Security Domains and Frameworks

This module explored the key areas covered by security frameworks such as NIST, ISO/IEC 27001, and CIS Controls. It helped me understand how to align security practices with business needs.

Module 2: Security Risk Management

This module introduced the risk management lifecycle—identify, assess, mitigate, and monitor. I practiced evaluating risk based on likelihood and impact and aligning controls with risk priorities.

Module 3: Business Continuity Planning and Disaster Recovery

I learned how organizations prepare for unexpected events that could disrupt operations. The module covered recovery strategies, backup planning, and the importance of recovery time objectives (RTOs).

Module 4: Security Assessment and Testing

This module covered different types of assessments, including vulnerability scans, penetration tests, and audits. I learned how assessments help organizations identify weaknesses and measure the effectiveness of security controls.
