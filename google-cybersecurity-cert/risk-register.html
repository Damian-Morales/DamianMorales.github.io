Risk Register Lab

Overview

In this lab, I was introduced to the purpose and structure of a cybersecurity risk register. I explored how organizations identify and manage risks to assets by assessing their likelihood, impact, and existing controls. The exercise included reviewing sample risk scenarios and documenting findings in a structured table.

Objective

Develop a working knowledge of how to create and interpret a cybersecurity risk register by evaluating common risks and suggesting relevant mitigation strategies.

Completed Risk Register Table

Asset Risk Likelihood Impact Risk Rating Safeguards
Employee Data Unauthorized access due to weak passwords High High High Enforce strong password policies and two-factor authentication
Customer Records Phishing attack leading to data breach Medium High Medium Security awareness training and email filtering tools
Internal Network Malware infection from external USB devices Low Medium Low Restrict use of external media and implement antivirus scanning

Conclusion

This exercise provided a clear understanding of how to prioritize cybersecurity risks and document them effectively. I now feel more confident in my ability to analyze risk scenarios, assess severity, and propose meaningful controls that support an organization's security posture.
