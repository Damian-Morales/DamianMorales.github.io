Foundations of Cybersecurity

Course Overview

This course introduces the core principles of cybersecurity, helping learners understand threats, roles, risk management strategies, and security tools essential for protecting digital systems and data.

Module 1: The World of Cybersecurity

This module explains what cybersecurity is, why it is important, and how organizations protect themselves. I am introduced to key terms such as confidentiality, integrity, and availability, and I explore the purpose of cybersecurity professionals.

Module 2: Understand the Cybersecurity Landscape

Examines the most common threats and vulnerabilities faced by individuals and organizations, such as malware, phishing, and insider threats. This module emphasizes the critical role of awareness and detection in prevention.

Module 3: Think Like a Cybersecurity Analyst

This module explores the day-to-day mindset and tasks of cybersecurity analysts. Learners understand how analysts investigate incidents, manage risks, and use frameworks like the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.

Module 4: Tools of the Trade

Introduced to common cybersecurity tools and technologies such as antivirus software, encryption, firewalls, and SIEM platforms. The module explains the purpose of each tool and how they work together to form layered defenses.
